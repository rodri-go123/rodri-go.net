My name is Rodrigo. I call myself a toolmaker within (graphic) design. This means that I strive to go beyond static end-results, designing interactive tools that can be publicly engaged with and processes that can be replicated.

My research interest lies in the philosophical, creative and social dimensions of digital technology and internet culture.

I am currently working on the Autonomous Web project, a series of publications searching for collaborative ways to practice autonomy on the internet.
