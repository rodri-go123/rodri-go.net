My name is Rodrigo and I am a toolmaker within (graphic) design. I strive to go beyond static end-results, designing tools that can be publicly engaged with and processes that can be replicated.

My interest lies in the philosophical, creative and social dimensions of digital technology and internet culture.

I work by questioning and exploring new ways of making, as I believe that crafting innovative design tools will always result in interesting outcomes. This, mixed with a taste for bold, vibrant colours, results in a body of work that is characterised by a heavy focus on process and a strong visual language.
